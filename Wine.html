Claire believes that Château de Vallois (CDV) is missing the new, emerging market of younger consumers because of its focus on super-premium, very high- priced wine.

She argues that a less expensive wine will open up a new market, gain valuable exposure for CDV, and increase revenues.

"What might happen if the estate allowed less expensive, lower quality wine makers particularly those from outside the traditional wine making regions of Europe to capture and retain the next generation of customers?"

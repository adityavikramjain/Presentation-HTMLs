Shall we go for an affordable wine?

A Strategic Dilemma for Château de Vallois

The Current Situation

  • Producers of the most premium wine in the world.
  • We sell in advance ("en primeur"), securing revenue and focus.
  • This allows the château to focus exclusively on world-class winemaking.
  • Our foundation is an excellent, trust-based relationship with trade partners.

The Core Conflict: Three Perspectives

Claire (The Innovator)

Believes we are missing the emerging younger market. Argues a less expensive wine will open new markets and grow revenue.

François (The Traditionalist)

Believes brand extension will severely undercut our perceived value and exclusivity in the marketplace.

Gaspard (The Guardian)

Must weigh expansion rewards against the risk of undermining a brand cultivated over centuries.

Preserve Luxury or Extend the Brand?

Current Strengths

  • World-class premium product
  • Financial stability from "en primeur"
  • Strong partner relationships

Threats & Opportunities

  • Stagnation due to capacity limits
  • Shifting market preferences
  • Dependence on middlemen
  • Distance from the end consumer

Force Field Analysis

Forces for Change

  • Revenue growth from new markets
  • Reduced dependence on Négociants
  • Closer connection to consumers
  • Build new marketing capabilities

Forces Against Change

  • Significant new investment required
  • Outside our core competence
  • Stretches management bandwidth
  • CRITICAL: Potential brand dilution

Who is our consumer?

And what are they truly paying for?

WHO: The Consumer

  • Able to discriminate quality

  • Collectors

Why Consumers Pay a Premium: The Luxury Seeker

WHO: The Consumer

  • Travels widely
  • Socialite / Networked
↔

WHAT: The Value

  • Status & Exclusivity

The Three Vs of Premium Wine

  • Valued Customers (WHO): Connoisseurs seeking taste, and Luxury Seekers pursuing status.
  • Value Proposition (WHAT): Meticulous detail, limited availability, and the masterful perception of luxury.
  • Value Chain (HOW): Every step controlled to maintain quality and mystique. Where is our advantage?

The Deciding Factor: Psychological Distance

Loading Chart...

Distant = Abstract 'Why'

rocket_launch

When something is psychologically distant, we focus on its core purpose, desirability, and the end goal. We ask: 'Why is this important?'

Near = Concrete 'How'

manage_search

When something is psychologically close, we focus on practical details, feasibility, and implementation. We ask: 'How does this work? How do I get it?'

The Customer Journey is a Map of Distance

Loading Chart...

Stage 1: Discovery = Maximum Psychological Distance

A new visitor is thinking in High-Level, abstract terms. They don't care about specs; they care about the benefit and the story. They are in the 'Why' mindset.

Stage 3: Purchase = Minimum Psychological Distance

A customer on your product page is about to act. They have shifted to a Low-Level, concrete mindset. They crave details: 'How big is it? What are the specs? How fast is shipping?' They are in the 'How' mindset.
